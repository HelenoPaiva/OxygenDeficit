Oxygen Deficit Calculator

Teaching-focused local tool to compare converted expired O₂ pressure with estimated arterial oxygen and the classical A–a gradient.

⚠️
Review these teaching assumptions:
  • Expired O₂ on the machine is entered as vol% and converted into partial pressure.
  • PETCO₂ is used as a practical surrogate for PaCO₂.
  • SpO₂ is converted to estimated PaO₂ using a simplified dissociation-curve model.
